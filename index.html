Message From Principal

Education of an individual starts at his / her birth and it continues throughout life and ultimate aim of this prestigious institute is to generate insatiable intellectual quest in young minds and to help them to understand themselves and the world around. It is envisaged that by providing students an environment with plenty of practical opportunities and encouragement we’ll be able to nurture all that is best for them, both morally and intellectually, so that when they leave Bahria College Karsaz to follow their future pursuits they find themselves equipped with knowledge, confidence, integrity and sense of duty.

— Captain Jamil Akhtar TI (M)
